Adrian Borja Chatbot's Privacy Policy

This is a simple chatbot application that will primarily serve as a proof of concept:

Information it can collect:

1) Your Facebook Firstname and Lastname 2) Free text value of your answer if you decided to do so to the Chatbot's non-personal questions

Information it cannot collect

1) Your Email Address 2) Any other information you do not want to give if in case the Chatbot asks you for it(non-personal questions only)
